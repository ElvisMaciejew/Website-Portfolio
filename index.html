About Me

Hi, my name is Elvis Maciejewski and I am a Global Business and Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo. I am currently in my second year and always learning new ways to improve my graphic design skills. I love designing posters, but I also have experience with creating video and computer games.

Previous Work

Over the course of my first two years of university, I have created many projects and am constantly improving my skills. These images showcase the process of how one of my favourite projects came to be. This project in particular was creating a poster of a specific genre, which I chose horror. I instantly knew the idea I wanted to go for and within all my projects, I tend to start off with a sketch to get a general idea of what It will look like. Then I took some of the pictures and once I chose the best one I began the editing process and created the finalized version!

Interests

One of my interests that I have always been passionate about was acting and modelling. My very first commercial I did was a drinking and driving commercial with MADD Canada back in June of 2019.
